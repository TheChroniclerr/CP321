COVID-19 Interactive Dashboard

Insights

The chart above shows the number of individuals who tested positive for COVID-19, died from it, and received vaccinations between 2020 and 2025 across different countries.

Missing values in the dataset were handled using zero-filling and then smoothed with a 3-month rolling mean.

Overall, there is a noticeable spike in new cases around 2023 and 2024, followed by a gradual decline into 2025, with India being a notable exception. Vaccination rates generally increase up to 2024 before declining, while new deaths do not display a clear overall trend. Note peaks and declines reflect real-world pandemic events, but correlation should not be interpreted as causation.
